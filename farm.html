Meet our mangoes

In Tanzania, mango is on the list of the top most consumed fruits i.e bananas, oranges, pineapples,mangoes and peas.Mango is traded and consumed as fresh or in processed form.

As of september 2020, Somango farm has about 130 Acres with over 10,000 grafted mango trees. Currently, the farm has three varieties namely Apple (20%), keitt(65%) and kent(15%).

Kent Mango

The original tree was grown from the seed planted arround 1925 on the property of Mrs. Victor mell of Miami, Florida. For the following decades palmer's parentage was unknown, however a 2005 pedigree analysis estimeted palmer was a seedling of Haden. The variety was first propagated in 1945 and officially named in 1949.

The fruit is large, with especially big specimens reaching severaal pounds in wight.

Keitt Mango

This open pollinated sedling of Mulgoba originated from Homestead (Florida) and was released in 1946.

The fruit is large with an average length of 11.7cm and a width of 9.2cm; it has an average weight of 456g, So the tree is medium sized, moderetely vigorous, producing long arching branches and has a scraggy open appearance.
