Who Are We

SoMango CO. Ltd is a limited liability company formed under Tanzanians laws and regulations.It has been registered and possesses valid certificate from Business Registrations and Licensing Agency(BRELA), Tanzania Revenue Authority(TRA) and Association of Mango Growers Tanzania(AMAGRO).



Our Mission

To produce the best tasting and finest quality fruits and products for the local community and the rest of the world using sustainable farming methods so as to improve the health of its customers and its local community.



Our vision

To become an African's best mango producer and seller with positive impact to the society serves.
