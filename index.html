Welcome SoMango farm

Once the humble friendly natural natural forest farm, SoMango Farm is today a 400 Acres working farm with over 130 Acres already planted with 10, 000 mango trees. The natural forested farm was bought from the village local government in 2014 and has been developed into succesful limited liability company with main activities including farming, selling & distribution of raw produce as well as processed products.

sound Principle of SoMango

SoMango Company Ltd.farm is based on the sound principle of; 1.protecting the environment by effective and efficient use of natural resources while limiting waste and carbon footprint. 2.Support local community by providing fair and sound employment together with maximum support for local development projects esp. health and education. 3.Feeding local society and the rest of the world with most delicious and halthy products by minimizing/eliminating unhealthy chemical inputs and physical conditions.
